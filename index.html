Study Rooms

There are 17 group study rooms on every floor of the Library. Rooms 036 and 146 have adjustable height tables for person with disabilities. Room 134 has adaptive equipment for people with disabilities.

Students can book a room for up to two hours, on a first-come first-served basis, at the New Media desk, 2nd Floor Library or online. There is a two-person minimum for group study rooms.

Brooklyn College students and CUNY Faculty are welcome to use our group study rooms for study, group work, online classes, job interviews, etc.

Room keys loaned out for up to two hours from the Circulation Desk (2nd floor). Reservations are held for 15 minutes after start time (and then canceled after that). Fines may accrue for keys returned late (see “Rules for Reservable Rooms”).
